# 👨‍🍳 ChefGPT – AI Powered Smart Pantry & Recipe Assistant

ChefGPT is an AI-powered cooking assistant that helps users manage their kitchen pantry and discover recipes based on the ingredients they already have.

By combining **AI image recognition, intelligent recipe generation, and smart pantry management**, ChefGPT helps users decide what to cook instantly. Users can scan pantry images, manually add ingredients, search recipes by category or cuisine, save favorite recipes, and even download recipe PDFs.

ChefGPT uses **Google Gemini AI** to generate recipes with **step-by-step cooking instructions**, making it easier for anyone to cook delicious meals.

# 🧠 How ChefGPT Works

ChefGPT simplifies cooking decisions in a few intelligent steps:

1️⃣ **Add or Scan Ingredients**
- Upload an image of your pantry or fridge
- Or manually add ingredients to your pantry

2️⃣ **AI Ingredient Detection**
- Google Gemini Vision analyzes the uploaded image
- Detects ingredients with estimated quantities

3️⃣ **Pantry Management**
- Ingredients are stored securely in the user’s personal pantry
- Users can edit or delete pantry items anytime

4️⃣ **AI Recipe Generation**
- Gemini AI analyzes available ingredients
- Generates recipes with step-by-step cooking instructions

5️⃣ **Save & Access Recipes**
- Users can save their favorite recipes
- Instantly access them later from the **My Recipes** page
